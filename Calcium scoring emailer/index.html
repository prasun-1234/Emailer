What is calcium scoring test?
A Calcium Scoring Test is a quick, painless CT scan that detects calcium buildup in your heart's arteries.
This buildup indicates plaque, which can lead to heart disease, and the test helps determine your individual risk.

Why get a Calcium Scoring Test?
Detects calcium buildup (plaque) in your arteries even before symptoms appear. Early detection allows for timely intervention and preventive measures to reduce your risk of heart attack or stroke.
